Iron Johns on Broadway

Explore our menus

Iron John's is where wood-fired pizza and delicious chargrilled burgers meet locally crafted beer. Our menu is built around bold flavors — from freshly rolled dough topped with a cinnamon sugar butter or freshly shaved parmesan and garlic, to hand-stretched pies, smash burgers, and rotating craft brews created by our very own head brewer, Elise Laure. Our lounge space was designed and built with passion by the hands of the owners, who wished to create a lively, industrial space unlike anything else in Tucson.
